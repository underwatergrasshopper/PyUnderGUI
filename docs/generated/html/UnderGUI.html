draw(view_range, texture_range, tint=<UnderGUI.Color.ColorF object>)¶

Draws fragment of texture into region in window client area. By default, Range is corresponding to (left, bottom, right, top).

Parameters

  • view_range (UnderGUI.Range) – Coordinates of region in window client area. Coordinates are normalized to range from 0.0 to 1.0. Max span is Range(0, 0, 1, 1).

  • texture_range (UnderGUI.Range) – Coordinates of region in texture. Coordinates are normalized to range from 0.0 to 1.0. Max span is Range(0, 0, 1, 1).

  • tint (UnderGUI.ColorF or UnderGUI.ColorB or UnderGUI.ColorI) – Modulates color of drawn texture fragment.
